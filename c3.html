Best business jobs to consider
The best business jobs combine good pay, job flexibility, career growth and a strong occupation outlook. The job outlook data outlined in this section is obtained from BLS. Whether you're a recent graduate or looking for a managerial job, here are 15 of the best business jobs for you to consider:


1. Receptionist
Primary duties: Receptionists perform clerical tasks in an office. They support clients by answering their questions, resolving issues, handling orders and sharing information about the organization's products and services. In most businesses, receptionists receive phone calls and redirect them to specific departments.

2. Office manager
Primary duties: Office managers fill several administrative roles for a company, including human resources, accounting and administration. An office manager supports the business by managing day-to-day operations in an office and undertaking administrative tasks, including supervising employees and organizing staff meetings. Their core duties include creating and maintaining schedules for staff, managing employees and monitoring payroll.

3. Public relations specialist
>Primary duties: Public relations specialists create and maintain the identity and image of a brand. Their tasks include managing communications across all channels, publishing press releases, providing answers to media inquisitions and monitoring the public's opinion about a company or individual. In the business arena, a public relations specialist is vital, ensuring that a company maintains a good reputation.

Animators may work on staff for movie and television production companies, collaborating with fellow artists to make a show. 5. Business coordinators organize
Business coordinators organize, manage and facilitate planning, monitoring and implementation of events and projects in an organization. Their tasks include streamlining project delivery by communicating details to all teams, collaborating with all stakeholders and managing deadlines and progress of key projects.
