Science and technology

Top technology and science jobs
If you are looking for a career in the technology and science field, consider this list of jobs with their annual average salaries and primary job duties:


1. Computer support specialist
Computer support specialists assist customers with computer related problems. By asking customers questions, these specialists are able to diagnose technical issues and explain any necessary steps the customer needs to take.

2. Veterinary technician
Veterinary technicians assist veterinarians in the care of animals being examined for injury or illness. They perform tasks such as taking diagnostic X-rays, administering medications and reporting conditions of the animals.

3. Avionics technician
Avionics technicians conduct maintenance on flying vehicles. Tasks include repairing components of the aircraft, replacing defective parts and reading maintenance manuals to educate themselves on repair procedures.

4. Chemical technician
Chemical technicians help chemists and engineers research, develop, produce and test chemical products and processes. They prepare chemical solutions and collect their findings in formal reports.

5. Environmental science technician
Environmental science technicians conduct laboratory and field tests to find sources of pollution. They maintain data collections of their findings and work to help solve environmental problems.
